About Us

Fluid Designs is a boutique UX & Creative Web Development company, working with global clients to create extraordinary and unique user experiences and designs. We create UX designs, build prototypes, build mobile apps, build web sites, and more.
Our process includes working closely with our clients to help them clarify and nail down their product vision and ideas into UX designs and create wireframes. Our process is in-depth for creating exceptional service to our clients.

  • Informational Analysis - Includes market and consumer research, stakeholder interviews to deliver use cases, user stories and behavioral scenarios.
  • Prototyping - Includes UX design, usability testing and concept validation by delivering wireframes, user flow diagrams and possibly interactive prototypes.
  • Visual Design - Includes the look and feel of the product by delivering the final product design which includes app logo, icons, and screenshots for app stores.
Our previous experience has ranged from consumer offerings to behind the scenes mission-critical enterprise projects.
